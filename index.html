Shigeru Miyamoto

Creator of the Mario and The Legend of Zelda video game franchises.

Some fun facts about Shigeru Miyamoto:

  • he plays the guitar, mandolin, and banjo
  • he has a wife and two children
  • he has a dog named Pikku
